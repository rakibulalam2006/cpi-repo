News & Events

Chittagong Polytechnic Institute is a well-known technical education institute in Bangladesh. It was established in 1962 and is located in Chattogram city. The institute offers diploma courses in various engineering technologies. It has skilled teachers and well-equipped laboratories. The institute plays an important role in developing skilled engineers for the country.
